NIFTY OFFSET

Promoting Artists ... Promoting Good

The Genesis

Framed is the genesis piece for Nifty Offset.

There are 3 coded or hidden messages within. One of the messages unlocks enough currency to offset 20 M/T of carbon or pay ETH gas fees for an artist who may need support in getting started. Alternatively, the finder could keep it and do nothing. The choice is theirs and that's the point.

Each day, we're faced with choices on how we'll interface with people and planet. As temperature and inequity rise, we can choose complacency or action.

If you've discovered the hidden currency and want to take action, here are a few we recommend:

  • Offset with Carbon Fund
  • Offset with Terra Pass
  • Reach out on Twitter or Clubhouse and offer to pay a new artist's gas fees
  • Support Education for Women and Girls

WHY OFFSET?

4° AND Inequity

We're on track for temperatures to rise by 4°C. The societal impact of hitting that grim target would be dramatic.

Proof-of-work consensus mechanisms cause non-trivial amounts of carbon emissions and proof-of-stake has potential to increase inequity.

Poor and marginalized communities who have little or no voice in the creation, implementation or scaling of these technologies will bear the brunt of the problems.

Nifty Offset's role is not only to promote artists and experiment with offsets but to understand, aggregate, and promote the arguments for and against technologies and platforms that one may wish to consider when choosing to mint or hold NFTs.
